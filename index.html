Concentration

The purpose of the game is to test your memory and ability to concentrate. You will be shown three images, flashed at you at different intervals. Remeber the image, and the sequence you were shown them. Next you will see all the possible images, click on the ones you were shown, in the same sequence you were shown them. You must remember at least 2 correctly to win. WHen you have finished selecting the three images, tap on the "Check Results" button for your score. To play again, but tap Replay If you're ready to begin, just tap the "Start Game" button below. Be ready, it begins immediatly. Have fun.
