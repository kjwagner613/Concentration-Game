Concentration

Instructions

  • This is a game about memory and concentration. You will be shown three random images at different intervals. They will only be up a fraction of a second, and they will not be shown again.

  • Remember the images and the sequence in which you saw them.

  • After you are presented with your three images, a grid of all twelve available images will be laid out to test your success.

  • As soon as the quiz of twelve images have been laid out, begin selecting the images that look like yours, and in the same order that you saw them.

  • When you click your images, make sure you see the button depress. You will know when you have clicked a button by the button's border. It will change colors when successfuly clicked.

  • You must select at least two images in their correct sequence, and make your selections in under TEN SECONDS, to win.

  • The Check Results button will appear once you have completed your third image selection. You can click on it to get your score.

  • If you would like to play again, just click the Play Again button at the end.

  • When you're ready to begin, go ahead and click the Start Game button below.

  • Be ready! The game begins as soon as you click that button. Have fun.
