Concentration Game Instructions

This is a game about memory and concentration. You will be shown three random images at different intervals. They will only be up a fraction of a second, and they will not be shown again.

Remember the images and the sequence in which you saw them. After you are presented with your three images, a grid of all twelve available images will be laid out to test your recall. As soon as the quiz of twelve images have been laid out, begin selecting the images that look like yours, and in the same order that you saw them. When you click your images, make sure you see the button activate which can be confirmed by the now blue border.

You must select at least two images in their correct position, and make your selections in under TEN SECONDS, to win. You can not change your selection once made. Your results will be displayed after you have clicked on your third image.

If you would like to play again, just click the Play Again button at the end. When you're ready to begin, go ahead and click the Start Game button below.

Hint: Ten seconds is not long, when you're looking for the images in the quiz, remember the locations of the others on the way. Be ready! The game begins as soon as you click that button.

Have fun.
